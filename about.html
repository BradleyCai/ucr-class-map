What is UCR Schedule Visualizer?

This website is a tool to kiss your frog of a schedule and turn him into a beautiful prince. We'll interpret your schdule and give you an hour by hour view as well as provide links to the buildings where your classes are.

Privacy Policy

All processing and thinking is done on your own computer. Nothing schedule related is sent to us. At all. We have no interest in selling your personal data to advertisers. You can even try disconnecting your internet and entering your data. If you're still skeptical, you can see all the source code on our GitHub. We do however store your GROWL schedule in a cookie that either expires after 10 years or when you click the button "Visualize Again".

We also collect statistics about who visits our page. The Akari Link Shortener records the clicks for our shortened link. The click count can be found here. Also, GitHub collects statistics about our general traffic and that can be found here.

Google Calendar integration requires an internet connection in order to send your schedule to Google so that the courses may be added to your calendar as events. For this, Google requires app developers to register for an API key with them and users to authorize the app that corresponds to that API key in order to grant privileges to modify the user's calendar. The API key for this app is registered to Joel Gomez and is only authorized for requests from specific domains.

If you'd like to get your own API key so that you may run this application on your own server, instructions are available here. Simply replace the key in the resources/api.json [file] (resources/api.json) with your own, make sure the domain for your project is registered to your key, and you're set.

This app will also make a separate API request to https://ucr-term-dates.appspot.com in order to get the start and end times for every quarter so that the dates for the Google Calendar events are reflective of the quarter's first and last day of instruction for your convenience. This API is also open source and the project can be found here.

If we ever change this privacy policy we will announce it on the alert section on the main page.
