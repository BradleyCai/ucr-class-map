UCR Schedule Visualizer

Copy your schedule, drop it down below,
and we'll draw out the hour by hour view and locations

What is this anyway? This website is a tool to kiss your frog of a schedule and turn him into a beautiful prince. We'll interpret your schdule and give you an hour by hour view as well as provide links to the buildings where your classes are at.

I don't want to give my schedule to some stranger though... Don't worry about it! All processing and thinking is done on your own computer. Nothing is sent to us. Try disconnecting your internet and entering your data. If you're still skeptical, you can see all the source code on our GitHub which you can find at the bottom of the page.
