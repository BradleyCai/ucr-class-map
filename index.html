What is this anyway? This website is a tool to kiss your frog of a schedule and turn him into a beautiful prince. We will interpret your schdule and give you a calender like view of it and provide links to the buildings where your classes are at.

I don't want to give my schedule to a stranger. Don't worry about it! All processing and thinking is done on your own computer. Nothing is sent to us. Try disconnecting your internet and entering your data. If you're still skeptical, you can see all the source code on our GitHub project located here.
